17th Annual Rocky Mountain Woodturning Symposium
 September 16th - 18th, 2016

sponsored by: Rocky Mountain Woodturners
a chapter of the American Association of Woodturners


Featured Demonstrators: David Ellsworth, Nick Cook, John Jordan, Jay Shepard, Les Brandt, Keith Gotschall, Michael Andersen, Ron Ainge, Larry Fox, Milo Scott, Martin Christensen, Joe Fleming,


The venue is the The Ranch / Larimer County Fair Grounds in Loveland, Colorado. Event will be in the McKee building, please use the free parking lots A, B & H


2016 marks the 17th year for this event. Participants can select from over 42 rotations given by some of the best woodturners in the world. The presentations will have something for everyone, from the beginner to the professional. There will be a large vendor exhibit and a hands on woodturning program area. An Instant Gallery will be open to the Public and all attendees are encouraged to bring 3 peices for exhibit. The symposium is open to anyone with an interest in woodturning and is the best value available.
