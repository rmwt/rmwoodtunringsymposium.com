Texture, Color, and Finish Turned Objects

Learn how to add color, bleach, and texture in ways that enhance the natural beauty of wood-turned objects. Dying, liming wax, bleaching, milk paints, acrylics, carving, and 3-D stippling will be covered.

Turned Candleholders, Sculpture, Pods

A faceplate-turned disc can be transformed into a unique two-part candleholder or a sculpture. A pod, turned from green wood, is a starting point for a box, a boat, or a sculpture. I will turn a disc and a pod, and then demonstrate safe methods for cutting apart turned objects using the bandsaw.

PowerPoint Discussion on Design and Creativity

Are you searching for a new perspective for designing your woodturnings? In this lively, interactive presentation, we will discuss design options while viewing a variety of work from other woodturners. These exercises will help you understand why approaching woodturning with a new outlook can be rewarding and fun!
