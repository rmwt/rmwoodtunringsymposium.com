18th Annual Rocky Mountain Woodturning Symposium
 September 15th - 17th, 2017

sponsored by: Rocky Mountain Woodturners
a chapter of the American Association of Woodturners


Featured Demonstrators:

Stuart Batty, Cindy Drozda, Cynthia Carden Gibson, Keith Gotschall, Kurt Hertzog, Eric Lofstrom, Jonathan Medina, Rick Orr, Curt Theobald and Tom Wirsing


The venue is the The Ranch / Larimer County Fair Grounds in Loveland, Colorado. Event will be in the McKee building, please use the free parking lots A, B & H


2017 marks the 18th year for this event. Participants can select from over 42 rotations given by some of the best woodturners in the world. The presentations will have something for everyone, from the beginner to the professional. There will be a large vendor exhibit and a hands on woodturning program area. An Instant Gallery will be open to the Public and all attendees are encouraged to bring 3 peices for exhibit. The symposium is open to anyone with an interest in woodturning and is the best value available.
