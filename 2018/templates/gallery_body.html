Instant Gallery

All attendees are encouraged to bring up to 3 pieces for exhibit at the Instant Gallery. The gallery will be open to the public, and your pieces can be displayed for sale or just for show.

The symposium will have some display pedestals available, or you may bring your own. We also provide a monitor to asist the public with sales, and to just watch over the display.

On the final morning of the symposium, demonstrators will do a critique session of selected gallery pieces.
