Dale Bonertz - Rough Out Bowls

For the past 20 years, Dale Bonertz has been self-employed as a remodeling contractor. He has an extensive background in wood window and door manufacturing and production projects through his remodeling business. He has been wood turning for eleven years and is a member of the AAW and Rocky Mountain Woodturners. He is most recognized through his involvement with roughing out bowls with his mentor and friend, Dan Bailey. His interest in production is constantly being refined and updated with the goal of becoming more efficient, quicker, and more effective with his turning.
