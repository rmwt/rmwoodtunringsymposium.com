Instructions

Use the forms below to enter your registration information and any meals that you desire. Then click on "Add to Cart" in each category. Use the "Continue Shopping" button to return to this page. When your registration is complete, use the "View Cart" button to be taken to the payment page.

Extra banquet meals are optional, so if you do not desire them then you can skip that section (do not click "Add to Cart").

If you desire multiples of any item, you can change the quantity in the Shopping Cart. Carefully review your items and then use the "Check Out" button.
